# TASK::TASK_VEHICLE_AIM_AT_PED

## Summary
The ped will aim from a vehicle the other ped passed.
Using:
Driveby weapons
Vehicle mounted weapons
Depending on what is currently selected in their inventory.
SEE ALSO TASK_VEHICLE_AIM_AT_COORD

## Parameters
* **PED_INDEX** pedIndex
* **PED_INDEX** otherPed
