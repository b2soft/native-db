# PED::APPLY_PED_DAMAGE_DECAL

## Summary
Applies a ped damage decoration (decal) to the ped, using the data defined in the peddamage.xml tuning file.
the decal could be a tatoo, dirt a scar, etc.

## Parameters
* **PED_INDEX** pedIndex: The index of the ped to apply the decoration decal to
* **PED_DECORATION_ZONE** zone
* **FLOAT** u: & v - the UV texture coords for the placement of the decal within the specified zone.
* **FLOAT** v
* **FLOAT** rotation: The rotation of the decoration blit around it's center, in degrees.
* **FLOAT** scale:
The scale of the decoration blit.
(range is 0.0 to 1.0, based on the Size tuning paramter in peddamage.xml)
* **FLOAT** alpha: _param - the alpha value use when applying the decal
* **INT** forceFrame: Specify a specific frame fromthe texture grid.
* **BOOL** fadeIn:
Should the decal fade in or just "pop" on decorationName - the name of the decal.
This should match a CPedDamageDecalInfo entry in build\dev\common\data\effects\peddamage.xml
* **STRING** damageDecalName
