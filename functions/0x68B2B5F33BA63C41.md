# CAM::POINT_CAM_AT_PED_BONE

## Summary
Points the camera at a ped's bone.

## Parameters
* **CAMERA_INDEX** cameraIndex
* **PED_INDEX** pedIndex: The index of the ped to point the camera at.
* **PED_BONETAG** boneTag:
The tag of the ped bone to point at.
See the definition of PED_BONETAG for further information.
vecOffset: An additional offset to be applied from the look-at position.
* **FLOAT** vecOffsetX
* **FLOAT** vecOffsetY
* **FLOAT** vecOffsetZ
* **BOOL** offsetIsRelative = true: If true, vecOffset is applied relative to the orientation of the ped being pointed at (not the bone), rather than in world-space.
